## Why it is no longer the universal production model

The exact-path approach requires a fully approved complete path for every supported Chamber combination. It does not provide enough surface variation for a larger resonance system without multiplying complete prewritten poems.

- the canonical examples;
- short-form rhythm and compression;
- structural validation concepts;
- poetic lineage for the V0.3 compact composer;
- legacy compatibility while current imports and tests still depend on this directory.
